About Me

Aryo currently holds the position of Senior Data Scientist at Epigene Labs (a precision oncology startup based in Paris, France). He focuses on the research of robust NLP-based solutions for clinical data aggregation, restructuring, and enrichment.

He has experienced both industry and research settings of AI. In the final year of his Bachelor's degree, he contributed to his advisor's research in Argumentation Mining where he managed to co-authored multiple conference papers.

Immediately after, Aryo joined Julo (a leading Indonesian financial technology company) to jumpstart his data science career. He moved to Paris in late 2018 to join Riminder (AI-driven Human Resource startup, now HrFlow) where he got quickly promoted to lead the AI research team.

With his deep expertise in Deep Learning and Natural Language Processing, he is set to revolutionize the process of extracting meaningful information from clinical data, especially in the context of precision oncology.
