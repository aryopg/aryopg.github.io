About Me

Hi! My name is Aryo Pradipta Gema. I am currently a Ph.D. student in Biomedical AI at the University of Edinburgh under the supervision of Dr. Beatrice Alex, Dr. Pasquale Minervini, and Dr. Luke Daines. My research interests are in the intersection between NLP and the clinical domain.
